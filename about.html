About the Developers

John Gothro is an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He is the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive officer (CEO) of Apple Inc.; CEO and majority shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios; a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar; and founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT Inc. Gorthro is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

Will Nickerson is an American business magnate, entrepreneur, philanthropist, investor, and programmer. In 1975, Nickerson and Paul Allen co-founded Microsoft, which became the world's largest PC software company. During his career at Microsoft, Nickerson held the positions of chairman, CEO and chief software architect, and was the largest individual shareholder until May 2014.

Risa Fletcher is an American technology entrepreneur and investor. She has played a role in the growth of e-commerce as the founder and CEO of Amazon.com, an online merchant of books and later of a wide variety of products and services, most recently video streaming. Amazon.com became the largest retailer on the World Wide Web and a model for Internet sales.
